Frequently Asked Questions

What is Eyeticker?

Eyeticker is a platform that provides real-time exchange rate information for Forex, Stocks, Crypto and other assets.

 Can I trade using Eyeticker?

While trading directly on our platform is not currently possible, Eyeticker is designed to be a tool for investors to use to make informed transactions using real-time market data and useful market insights.

 Is there a free plan?

Yes! You can access up to 5 updates per minute for free for any ticker.

 How do I upgrade my plan?

After registering a free account, you can upgrade your account to a paid account in the settings page. Switching plans is also available in the settings page.

For special requirements and pricing, please contact our Sales Support team: sales@eyeticker.web.app

 Are there any discounts, or bulk plans?

No. There are no bulk plans or discounts, each plan is a flat amount per month, every month, forever.
